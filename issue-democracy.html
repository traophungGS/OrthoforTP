Democracy & Voting Rights

Protecting the Foundation of Our Republic

Democracy is Under Attack

Voter suppression, gerrymandering, and the erosion of voting rights threaten the very foundation of our democracy. Ortho is committed to strengthening democratic institutions and ensuring every eligible American has their voice heard at the ballot box.

Key Initiatives

Voting Rights Protection

We will restore and strengthen the Voting Rights Act, end voter suppression tactics, and ensure universal access to the ballot. Every eligible citizen deserves the right to vote without barriers.

End Gerrymandering

Gerrymandering distorts representation and undermines democracy. We support independent redistricting commissions and fair voting districts that accurately reflect communities.

Expand Early Voting & Mail-In Ballots

Make voting convenient and accessible for everyone. Early voting, mail-in ballots, and same-day registration should be the standard, not the exception.

Campaign Finance Reform

Money shouldn't determine elections. We support public financing of campaigns and strict limits on corporate money in politics.

Restore Voting Rights for Felons

People who have served their time should be able to participate fully in democracy. We support restoring voting rights to formerly incarcerated individuals.

Protect Election Security

Elections must be secure and transparent. We support paper ballots, regular audits, and protection against foreign interference.

Why Democracy Matters

Our democracy is only as strong as our commitment to it. When we suppress votes, we suppress voices. When we allow money to dominate politics, we allow the powerful to dominate our government. It's time to reclaim our democracy for the people.

Our Commitment

Ortho will:

  • Pass comprehensive voting rights legislation
  • Strengthen election security and transparency
  • Fight voter suppression in all its forms
  • Build a democracy that serves all people, not just the wealthy

Protect Our Democracy

Democracy requires participation. Join us in the fight to strengthen voting rights and protect our republic.
