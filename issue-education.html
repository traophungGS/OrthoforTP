Education Access

Investing in Our Future Through Quality Education

Education is the Great Equalizer

Quality education opens doors and creates opportunity. Yet too many students are locked out by cost, inequality, and underfunded schools. Ortho believes every child deserves access to excellent education, from pre-K through college.

Key Initiatives

Free Public College & Trade School

Education shouldn't come with crushing debt. We support free tuition at public colleges and universities, as well as vocational and trade schools.

Universal Pre-K & Childcare

Give every child a strong start. We support universal access to pre-K and affordable childcare so parents can work and children can learn.

Fund Our Schools Fairly

School funding shouldn't depend on zip code. We support equitable funding for all schools, with additional resources for schools serving low-income students.

Support Teachers

Teachers are the backbone of education. We support fair wages, benefits, and working conditions for teachers.

Student Debt Relief

Millions of Americans are crippled by student debt. We support debt forgiveness and making future education affordable.

Expand STEM & Arts Education

Science, technology, engineering, math, and arts education prepare students for the future. We support these critical areas.

The Investment Payoff

Investing in education isn't an expense—it's an investment that pays dividends for generations. Educated workers earn more, innovate more, and contribute more to their communities. When we invest in education, we invest in our entire nation's future.

Our Education Vision

Ortho will:

  • Make education free and accessible for all
  • Fund schools equitably across all communities
  • Support teachers with fair compensation
  • Prepare students for the jobs of tomorrow

Invest in Our Future

Education changes lives. Join us in the fight for accessible, quality education for all.
