Meet Ortho

Ortho is dedicated to building a government that serves the people—committed to social justice, democratic values, and a brighter future for all. With a vision rooted in equality and transparency, we're fighting for policies that matter to everyday Americans.

Where Ortho Stands on the Issues

Social Justice

Equal rights and opportunities for all, regardless of background or identity. We're committed to dismantling systemic inequalities and building a more equitable society.

Democracy & Voting Rights

Protecting the right to vote and strengthening democratic institutions. Every voice matters, and every vote counts in building our future together.

Economic Opportunity

Creating good-paying jobs and accessible economic opportunities for working families. An economy that works for everyone, not just the few at the top.

Healthcare for All

Ensuring quality healthcare is accessible and affordable. Everyone deserves the ability to see a doctor and get the care they need.

Climate & Environment

Protecting our planet while creating green jobs. We must act now to combat climate change and ensure a sustainable future for generations to come.

Education Access

Making quality education affordable and accessible. Investing in our schools and students is investing in our nation's future.
