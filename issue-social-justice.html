Social Justice

Building an Equitable Society for All

Our Commitment to Social Justice

Ortho believes that true progress cannot be made until we address the systemic inequalities that have held back millions of Americans. Social justice is not just a political priority—it's the foundation of everything we fight for.

Key Initiatives

Criminal Justice Reform

We are committed to reforming a criminal justice system that has disproportionately targeted communities of color. This includes ending mass incarceration, reforming policing practices, and ensuring equitable treatment for all.

Racial Equity

Addressing systemic racism in housing, employment, education, and healthcare. We support reparations discussions and policies that actively work to close the racial wealth gap.

LGBTQ+ Rights

Full protection and equality for the LGBTQ+ community, including workplace protections, housing rights, and access to healthcare. We stand firmly against discrimination in all its forms.

Women's Rights

Protecting reproductive freedom, ensuring pay equity, and ending workplace harassment and discrimination. Women deserve full autonomy over their bodies and futures.

Immigrant Justice

We believe America is stronger with immigrants. We support pathways to citizenship, humane immigration policies, and protection for all workers regardless of status.

Disability Rights

Ensuring accessibility, inclusion, and equal opportunities for people with disabilities. Everyone deserves to participate fully in society.

Why This Matters

For too long, marginalized communities have been left behind while the powerful few benefited from systems rigged in their favor. Social justice isn't about special treatment—it's about fairness, equity, and ensuring everyone has an equal shot at the American dream.

Our Path Forward

Ortho will:

  • Implement evidence-based policies that address systemic inequality
  • Listen to and amplify the voices of affected communities
  • Hold government accountable for equity outcomes
  • Build coalitions across movements to create lasting change
