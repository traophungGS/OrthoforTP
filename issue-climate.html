Climate & Environment

Protecting Our Planet and Creating Green Jobs

Climate Change is the Challenge of Our Time

We have the technology and resources to address climate change. What we need is the political will. Ortho is committed to aggressive climate action that protects our planet while creating millions of good-paying green jobs.

Key Initiatives

Green New Deal

Invest in clean energy, green infrastructure, and sustainable transportation. Create millions of good-paying jobs while transitioning to a clean energy economy.

100% Clean Energy by 2035

Set aggressive targets for renewable energy. Move away from fossil fuels and transition to solar, wind, and other clean energy sources.

Protect Our Communities

Environmental injustice disproportionately affects low-income and communities of color. We support environmental remediation and relocation support where needed.

Green Transportation

Invest in public transit, electric vehicles, and sustainable transportation infrastructure to reduce emissions.

Conservation & Public Lands

Protect our forests, wetlands, and public lands. Support conservation efforts and protect against corporate exploitation.

Hold Polluters Accountable

Corporations that pollute our air and water must be held accountable. We support strict environmental regulations and enforcement.

Economic Opportunity Through Green Jobs

The transition to clean energy isn't a burden—it's an opportunity. Green jobs are often local, well-paying, and can't be outsourced. By investing in renewable energy and sustainable infrastructure, we create millions of jobs while protecting our planet.

Our Environmental Vision

Ortho will:

  • Treat climate change with the urgency it deserves
  • Transition to 100% clean energy
  • Create millions of green jobs
  • Protect our environment and environmental justice

Fight for Our Planet

Our planet's future depends on action today. Join us in the fight for climate justice.
