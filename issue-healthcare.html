Healthcare for All

Healthcare as a Human Right, Not a Luxury

Healthcare is a Right

In the wealthiest nation on Earth, no one should go bankrupt because they get sick. Ortho believes healthcare is a human right, not a privilege for the wealthy. We are committed to ensuring affordable, accessible healthcare for all Americans.

Key Initiatives

Medicare for All

We support a single-payer healthcare system where every American is guaranteed comprehensive coverage. No more premiums, deductibles, or fighting with insurance companies.

Lower Drug Prices

Pharmaceutical companies charge Americans far more for the same drugs sold in other countries. We support allowing Medicare to negotiate drug prices and importing affordable medications.

Expand Mental Health Services

Mental health is health. We support expanding access to mental health services, counseling, and addiction treatment.

Reproductive Health & Rights

Access to reproductive healthcare, including contraception and abortion, should be available to everyone who needs it.

Preventive Care & Wellness

An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure. We support investments in preventive care and wellness programs.

Address Health Disparities

Communities of color and low-income communities face worse health outcomes. We support targeted efforts to address systemic health disparities.

The Cost of Inaction

Our current healthcare system is bankrupting families and letting people die from preventable diseases. It's time for a system that puts people first, not profits.

Our Healthcare Vision

Ortho will:

  • Guarantee healthcare to every American
  • Lower drug and healthcare costs
  • Expand access to mental health services
  • Protect reproductive rights and healthcare

Fight for Healthcare for All

Everyone deserves quality healthcare. Join us in the fight for a healthier America.
