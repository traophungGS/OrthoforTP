Meet Ortho

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Who is Ortho?

Ortho is a committed leader dedicated to building a government that truly serves the people. With deep roots in community organizing and a passion for progressive change, Ortho brings a fresh perspective to politics—one that prioritizes equity, transparency, and the voices of everyday Americans.

Our Mission

To create a more just, equitable, and democratic society where every person has the opportunity to thrive, where their voices are heard, and where government works for everyone, not just the privileged few.

Our Core Values

Justice

We believe in dismantling systemic inequalities and building structures that protect and uplift marginalized communities.

Democracy

We are committed to strengthening democratic institutions and ensuring every voice has equal power in shaping our future.

Transparency

We operate with complete openness and accountability, making decisions in partnership with the communities we serve.

Inclusivity

We celebrate our differences and recognize that our collective strength comes from our diversity and unity.

Why Ortho?

The current political system has failed too many people. It's time for leadership that truly listens, that puts people over corporate interests, and that has the courage to fight for the changes our communities deserve. Ortho represents a new vision for America—one where democracy isn't a promise, but a reality for everyone.
