Economic Opportunity

An Economy That Works for Everyone

Economic Justice for All

Working families are being squeezed from all sides while corporate profits soar and the wealthy hoard wealth. Ortho believes in an economy that creates good-paying jobs, provides opportunities for advancement, and ensures no one is left behind.

Key Initiatives

Raise the Minimum Wage

No one working full-time should live in poverty. We support raising the federal minimum wage to $15 per hour and indexing it to inflation.

Support Small Businesses & Worker Cooperatives

Small businesses are the backbone of our economy. We support grants, low-interest loans, and tax credits for small business owners and worker-owned cooperatives.

Strengthen Labor Rights

Workers deserve the right to organize, bargain collectively, and work in safe conditions. We support the right to unionize and enforce strong labor protections.

Job Training & Education Programs

We invest in apprenticeships, vocational training, and community colleges to ensure people have the skills for good-paying jobs in tomorrow's economy.

Close the Wage Gap

Women and people of color earn less for doing the same work. We support equal pay legislation and enforcement to close these unjust gaps.

Tax the Wealthy Fairly

The wealthy and large corporations pay less in taxes than they should. We support progressive taxation and closing tax loopholes.

The Path to Prosperity

An economy that works for everyone isn't a handout—it's smart economics. When working people have good jobs, they spend money in their communities, supporting local businesses and creating more jobs. When we close the wage gap and support worker rights, we strengthen the middle class.

Our Economic Vision

Ortho will:

  • Create millions of good-paying jobs
  • Strengthen the middle class and support working families
  • Support small businesses and worker cooperatives
  • Build an economy that serves people, not just profits

Build Economic Opportunity Together

Working families deserve a fair shot. Join our movement for an economy that works for everyone.
